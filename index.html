about THE HOUSE

I'm Becky Warren, a Nashville songwriter. I built this machine on a hunch: melodies show up when the stakes are low and the lights are warm.

Pull the lever, take the mood and the style the wheels deal you, and sing the couplet before you can overthink it. The band is loose and nobody here reads music either.

More songs at thisisbeckyw.substack.com.

shall we ROLL TAPE?

If you give the house your microphone, it records each number you sing — so any melody you stumble into is yours to keep. Takes go on the set list, where you can play them back or download them.

Everything stays on this device. Nothing leaves the room.

You can flip recording on or off anytime at the REC switch below the machine.
